Our Services

Professional cleaning for every surface — residential, commercial & industrial

Pressure Cleaning

High-pressure water blasting cuts through years of grime, mould, algae and dirt — restoring your surfaces to like-new condition without damaging them.

  • Driveways & footpaths
  • Patios, decks & pool areas
  • Fences & retaining walls
  • Commercial car parks

Acid Washing

Our professional acid washing service uses safe yet powerful solutions to deep clean concrete, brick, and tile — removing mineral stains and efflorescence that pressure cleaning can't touch.

  • Concrete & exposed aggregate
  • Brick & paver restoration
  • Tile & grout cleaning
  • Pool surrounds & coping

Oil & Rust Stain Removal

Specialised chemical treatments that break down and lift even the most stubborn oil spills, rust stains and industrial residues from concrete and paved surfaces.

  • Garage & driveway oil stains
  • Rust from metal fixtures
  • Industrial contamination
  • Tyre mark removal

Brick & Render Cleaning

Safe, low-pressure soft washing and specialist solutions to clean rendered walls, face bricks and sandstone without causing any damage or surface erosion.

  • Rendered home exteriors
  • Face brick & heritage brick
  • Sandstone & limestone
  • Paint & graffiti removal

Driveway Restoration

Complete driveway makeover — from deep cleaning and stain removal to sealing and resurfacing. We restore your driveway to its original, pristine look.

  • Concrete driveways
  • Exposed aggregate
  • Paved & cobblestone
  • Sealing & protection

Industrial & Commercial

Large-scale cleaning solutions for businesses, industrial facilities, shopping centres and commercial properties. We work around your schedule to minimise disruption.

  • Warehouses & factories
  • Car parks & loading docks
  • Shopping centres
  • Grease trap & waste areas
